Lifetime Residuals

Lifetime Residuals are a crucial benefit for ISOs at Netevia Platinum, providing a sustainable income stream. As long as the clients you support continue processing transactions with Netevia, you'll consistently earn a portion of the revenue.

Vested Since Day 1

At Netevia Platinum, we prioritize immediate empowerment for our ISOs, showcased through our 'Vested Since Day 1' benefit. From day one, you have full ownership of the residuals from the accounts you onboard. For instance, if you secure an account that generates $40 monthly in residuals, you will continue to receive this income for as long as the account remains active, irrespective of whether you add more accounts.
